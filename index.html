WELCOME TO GUESS THE EMOJI

Inspired by Hues And Cues, Guess The Emoji is a board game developed to document words used by players to describe emojis. HOW TO PLAY

If playing in a 4+ player group, you can form 2-3 player teams, although this is not necessary! Players can play individually if desired.

The Manager will click any emoji 18 times (yes, i know, but just bare with me haha), making all 6 colored chips available.
